AB 495

This page has some comments about Assembly Bill 495.

Family institution's ability to pickup their child

Will parents who are already present and adequately raising their child be at risk of an unexpected person picking up their child?

Family was one of three institutions on earth along with Government and church according to the diagram below. Each of the institutions ideally has its leader. Would the bill dismantle the leader for a present family institution adequately taking care of their child?

The diagram was reconstructed from a video sermon at time 00:22:00. The symbol identifying the church institution was a key, Government was a sword, and family a rod. Scriptures for the diagram were copied to table 1.

No family code summary was observed in the California Code of Regulations (CCR) so the diagram seemed suitable to fill in. Family code did exist in the legislative codes, just not observed in the CCR.

Existing codes

By the code hierarchy in table 2, article 5.7 indicated the bill could respond to immigration situations. If a immigrant parent was going back to their home country, would California want to check if there was a family rule back in the home country? Assuming some home countries would want the child to stay with his/her parent, is that a good way to help the child receive care?

Local Child Services

Would local child support services have a harder time reporting? 22 CCR 121120 State Performance Measures had a report for (7) Percent of children for whom paternity has been established. Is it more confusing who the parent is if the custody rules are changed, or family institution modified?
